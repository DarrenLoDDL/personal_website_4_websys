Aesthetic

The website was designed with minimalism and aesthetic in mind. The colour pallete was chosen based on the images used for the footer and the banner. There aren't too many elements on a single page, and elements are mainly stacked one on top of the other, making the site easy to read and navigate through.

Fonts

The font that was used throughout the website is "Abel" which is provided by the Google Fonts API, with default Sans Serif, if anything goes wrong. I could say that I chose because of visual clarity and appeal, but really, I just liked the way it looked.

Accessibility

This site can be navigated by either mouse or keyboard. Since there aren't a lot of hyperlinks in the page, it is easy to access certain pages within a few presses of the tab button. The font sizes, word spacing, and letter spacing, ensire that the text on this site didnt look too squished, and may make it easier for users that are visually imapired in some way to read have an easier time reading the site. The colours on this site are not too bright (though they are mostly on the blue end of the spectrum of light) and should be nicer for users that live life in "Dark Mode". The text colours are all white or some denomination of white to stand out from the rest of the background.

Responsiveness

The site should be able to be accessed across different platforms, since the page should scale based on the viewport of the device. The user should also expierence no problems zooming into the site, as some elements have percentage based height and width. The links also respond quick enough for normal use. Images used on the site are around 500k, but no more, so loading should not be an issue
